About The India Cements

The India Cements is one of the leading cement manufacturing companies in India, with a strong presence in the southern part of the country. Since its inception, India Cements has grown to be a major player in the cement industry, driven by a commitment to quality and excellence.

Founded in 1946, India Cements has established itself as a household name and has significantly contributed to India’s infrastructure development. With a focus on sustainability and innovation, the company has expanded its reach to new markets and continually adapts to meet the growing demands of the construction industry.

Our History

The journey of The India Cements began in 1946, during the early days of India’s independence movement. With a vision to build a strong and self-reliant nation, India Cements has been a proud contributor to major infrastructure projects across the country. From laying the foundation for countless homes to shaping the skyline with high-rise buildings, India Cements’ products are trusted by builders, architects, and engineers alike.

Today, India Cements operates several plants across the country, producing high-quality cement products to cater to a wide range of construction needs. With a strong network and a dedicated workforce, the company continues to innovate, aiming to reduce its environmental footprint while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

Key Milestones:
  • 1946: Founded with a vision to contribute to India's growth
  • 1959: Established the first cement plant in Sankarnagar, Tamil Nadu
  • 1989: Expanded operations with additional plants across South India
  • 2000s: Focused on sustainable practices and modernized manufacturing processes
  • Present: A leading name in the cement industry with a strong emphasis on innovation and quality
